SPLoginViewControllerDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in SPLoginViewController.h

Overview

Provides a completion callback from SPLoginViewController. SPLoginViewController can cause multiple login and logout events during the login and signup process. This delegate informs you when the process is complete.
